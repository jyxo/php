Jyxo PHP Library

A long time ago, in a galaxy far far awayâ€¦ there has been an IT company called Jyxo s.r.o. It has existed for several years and created and contributed to numerous projects.

To share fruits of work of individual project teams and to not re-invent the wheel over and over again, a collection of libraries has been created. It has been given the name "Jyxo PHP Library" and it has happily grown throughout the years.

In our repositories we will continuously publish various bits and pieces from this library. We will do our best to publish everything that would be useful to anyone from the outside world. A big part of the library will also be covered by PHPUnit tests.

Please read the license before using anything from this library.

The library is published in two versions:

  • With namespaces (license, documentation).
  • Without namespaces (license, documentation).
